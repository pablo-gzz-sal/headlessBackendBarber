# Managing Product Collections - Joey's Guide

## Overview
Your site has three special product sections that you can easily manage:

1. **Joey's Faves** - Your personal favorites
2. **Sale** - Discounted products
3. **Bestsellers** - Most popular items

## How to Add Products to Collections

### Step 1: Open Shopify Admin
1. Log into your Shopify admin
2. Go to **Products** in the left sidebar

### Step 2: Edit a Product
1. Click on the product you want to feature
2. Scroll down to the **Tags** section

### Step 3: Add Tags
Add one or more of these tags:
- `joeys-faves` - Product appears in Joey's Faves
- `sale` - Product appears in Sale section
- `bestseller` - Product appears in Bestsellers

### Step 4: Save
Click **Save** - that's it! The product will automatically appear in the collection.

## Examples

### Making a Product a "Joey's Fave"
1. Edit the product
2. In Tags field, add: `joeys-faves`
3. Save

### Making a Product Both a Sale Item and Bestseller
1. Edit the product
2. In Tags field, add: `sale, bestseller`
3. Save

## Important Notes
- Products update in collections **automatically** when you change tags
- You can add/remove products anytime
- No developer needed!
- Changes appear on the website immediately
